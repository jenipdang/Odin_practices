4 Main Image Formats

Back to Homepage

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras information.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations.

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams, logos and icons.

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them whenever you can. There is one protential issue with SVGs: for them to display consistently across browswers, you need to convert any text fields to outlines using your image editor (e.g., Abode Illustrator or Sketch). If you images contain a lot of text, this can have a big impact on file size. For this reason, use PNGs intead of SVGs, even though SVGs are so awesome.
