French Dip Sandwich

Back

Description

A French dip sandwich that is quick and easy and the whole family will love.

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 2

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 548
  • Fat: 23g
  • Carbs: 41g
  • Proten: 45g

Ingredients

  • 4 Hoagie Rolls, Split Lengthwise
  • 1 (10.5 Ounce) Can Beef Consomme
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 Pound Thinly Sliced Deli Roast Beef
  • 8 Slices Provolone Cheese

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Open hoagie rolls and lay them out on a baking sheet.

Step 2

Heat beef consomm√© and water in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat to make a rich beef broth. Place roast beef in broth and warm for 3 minutes. Arrange meat on hoagie rolls and top each roll with 2 slices of provolone.

Step 3

Bake sandwiches in the preheated oven until cheese melts, about 5 minutes. Serve sandwiches with small bowls of warm broth for dipping.
